About

Erikson Aguiar is a Ph.D. student of Security and Privacy in Machine Learning at Database and Image Group (GBDI), University of São Paulo, Brazil. Her research interests include Machine Learning, Image Processing, Security & Privacy, Distributed Systems, and Blockchain. Currently, He has researched Security and Privacy in Machine Learning to aim to build Deep Learning models trustworthy for healthcare applications. Also, he has technical skills in C, Golang, Nodejs, Python, Git, TensorFlow, Pytorch, Scikit-learn, OpenCV, Docker, GCP, and AWS.

I am passionate about coffee, sports, and rock'n roll. When I leave the office I enjoy to cheer on Flamengo, watching nerd stuffs, and running.
